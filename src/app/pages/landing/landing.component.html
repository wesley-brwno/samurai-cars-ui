About

Welcome to Samurai's Cars, your one-stop destination for all your vehicle needs. Our website offers a wide range of vehicles for sale, from bikes to powerful tractors. We provide a platform for owners to advertise their vehicles by signing up on our website. Our user-friendly interface makes it easy to browse through our inventory and find the perfect car for you.

Roles and Access

The application has two roles for logged users: USER and ADMIN.

  • A USER can add and manage vehicles and access messages sent to them.
  • An ADMIN can do the same, and also manage USERs' vehicles if needed. In the future, an ADMIN will also be able to manage USERs directly.
